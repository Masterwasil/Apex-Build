A Legacy of Structured Execution

Construction can be complex, but it shouldn't be confusing. We break down the entire lifecycle of your project into manageable phases. This ensures you are always informed about the progress, budget, and timeline.

1. Consultation & Site Visit

Duration: 1 Week

We meet to understand your requirements, budget, and vision. Our engineers visit the site to assess soil conditions, plot dimensions, and local regulations. We provide a preliminary feasibility report.

2. Design & Estimation

Duration: 2-4 Weeks

Our architects create 2D floor plans and 3D elevations based on your inputs. Simultaneously, we prepare a detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ) giving you a precise cost estimate with no hidden charges.

3. Approvals & Agreement

Duration: Variable

We sign a formal construction agreement defining specifications, payment schedules, and timelines. We also assist in obtaining necessary sanctions from BBMP/BDA and other authorities.

4. Excavation & Structure

Duration: 3-6 Months

The ground is broken! We proceed with earthwork, footing, columns, and concrete roofing. Regular quality checks (cube tests) are conducted to ensure structural strength.

5. Brickwork & Plastering

Duration: 2-3 Months

Walls are raised, and internal/external plastering is completed. Electrical conduits and plumbing lines are concealed within the walls at this stage.

6. Finishing & Interiors

Duration: 2-3 Months

Flooring, painting, window installation, and carpentry work happen now. The building transforms into a livable space with premium finishes.

7. Quality Check & Handover

Duration: 2 Weeks

A final snags-list inspection is done. Once everything is perfect, we hand over the keys along with the structural warranty certificate and "As-Built" drawings.

Process FAQs

How do you handle payment schedules?

Payments are stage-wise. You pay a percentage upon completion of specific milestones (e.g., Plinth completion, 1st Slab, Plastering). This ensures your money is always safe.

Can I make changes during construction?

Yes, minor changes are accommodated. However, structural changes are difficult after casting. Any extra cost due to changes in material or scope will be billed separately.

Do you handle temporary electricity and water?

Yes, we assist in applying for temporary BESCOM and BWSSB connections required for construction. The consumption charges are usually borne by the client or as per the agreement.
