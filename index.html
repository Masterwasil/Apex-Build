Common Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Building a property is a big decision. Here are some common queries our clients have regarding timelines, costs, and approvals.

Can't find your answer? Contact Us

Do you handle BBMP plan approvals?

Yes, we provide end-to-end assistance including architectural drawings, structural vetting, and obtaining plan sanctions from BBMP, BDA, or BMRDA authorities.

What is the estimated cost of construction per sq ft?

Construction costs vary based on the quality of materials (Premium/Luxury) and design complexity. Generally, it ranges from ₹1800 to ₹2800+ per sq ft in Bangalore. Contact us for a precise quote.

Do you provide a warranty on construction?

Absolutely. We provide a 1-year general maintenance warranty and a 10-year warranty on the structural integrity of the building.
