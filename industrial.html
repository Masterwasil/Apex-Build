Built for Production & Logistics

Industrial projects require a different breed of expertise. At Apex Buildstone, we specialize in high-tolerance flooring, wide-span roofing, and heavy machinery foundations. Whether you need a Pre-Engineered Building (PEB) for rapid deployment or a specialized manufacturing plant, we ensure your facility is operational on schedule.

PEB Structures

Pre-Engineered Buildings are the future of industrial construction. We design and assemble steel structures that are cost-effective, durable, and expandable.

  • Rapid On-site Assembly
  • Large Clear Spans (up to 80m)
  • Weather-proof Roofing Systems

Manufacturing Plants

Custom-built facilities for textile, automotive, and FMCG sectors. We integrate specialized requirements like ETP/STP plants and boiler foundations.

  • Heavy Load Bearing Floors
  • Vibration Isolation Foundations
  • Utility & Pipe Rack Integration

Warehousing & Logistics

Grade-A warehousing solutions designed for modern supply chains. We focus on vertical storage efficiency and smooth material movement.

  • VDF (Vacuum Dewatered) Flooring
  • Automated Dock Levelers
  • 12m+ Eave Heights

Industrial Civil Works

Beyond the building, we handle the entire compound development including boundary walls, internal concrete roads, and storm water drains.

  • Concrete Road Networks
  • Underground Cabling
  • Security Infrastructure

Safety & Compliance

EHS Compliance

Strict adherence to Environment, Health, and Safety protocols. We enforce mandatory PPE and regular site audits.

Precision Engineering

Laser-guided leveling for flooring (FM2 standards) to ensure smooth operation of forklifts and robotics.

Timeline Guarantee

We understand that downtime costs money. Our parallel processing of civil and fabrication works speeds up delivery.

Factory Act Norms

Designs fully compliant with the Factories Act, 1948, ensuring hassle-free licensing and approvals.
