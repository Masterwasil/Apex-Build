Our Story

Building a Legacy of Trust

Discover the passion, precision, and people behind Bangalore's most reliable construction firm.

Since 1998

From Humble Beginnings to
Industry Leaders

Apex Buildstone began as a small family-run civil contracting firm in South Bangalore. Founded by Mr. Ramesh Gowda with a single mixer truck and a team of ten, we were driven by a simple promise: to deliver what we promise, on time.

Over the last two decades, Bangalore transformed from a Garden City to the Silicon Valley of India, and we evolved with it. We embraced modern construction technologies like Mivan shuttering and green building practices, allowing us to execute complex commercial and residential projects with precision.

"Construction is not just about brick and mortar; it's about creating spaces where life happens and businesses thrive."

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

Every year marks a new chapter of growth and engineering excellence. Here is a glimpse into the major milestones of Apex Buildstone.

1998

Inception

Established as 'Apex Contractors' in a small office in Jayanagar, focusing on residential renovations.

2005

First Commercial Project

Successfully delivered a 4-story commercial complex in Indiranagar, marking our entry into the commercial sector.

2010

Incorporation & Expansion

Rebranded to 'Apex Buildstone Pvt Ltd'. Expanded team to 50+ engineers and acquired heavy machinery fleet.

2015

ISO Certification

Awarded ISO 9001:2015 certification for Quality Management Systems. Reached the milestone of 100 completed projects.

2019

The Tech Park Era

Partnered to build a 250,000 sq. ft. IT Park in Whitefield, our largest project to date.

2023

Green Building Award

Recognized by the Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) for sustainable construction practices in our 'Emerald Villa' project.
